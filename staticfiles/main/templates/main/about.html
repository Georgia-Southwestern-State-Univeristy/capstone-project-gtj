About Us

Welcome to GTJ GO! Our mission is to make travel safer, more informed, and more enjoyable for everyone. GTJ GO! uses gathered safety data from a database of trusted statistics on women’s safety in many countries GTJ GO! also utilizes APIs to get accurate and up-to-date crime information​ Once information is gathered our algorithm then formulates an all-emcompasing safety score for a country​. However, GTJ GO! isn’t just for safety. It offers real-time information for​ Live language translations ​ Currency exchanges​ Packing guides​ Current weather and itinerary based on weather conditions​ Live transportation search and routes​ Cultural nuances, history, and facts​ Reviews from other users​
